✈️ A Whirlwind Escape to Istanbul, Türkiye 🕌🌉

Last month, I embarked on a magical journey to Istanbul, the city where two continents meet. From wandering through the ancient halls of the Hagia Sophia, to sipping authentic Turkish tea by the Bosphorus, every moment felt like a scene from a storybook. I explored the vibrant Grand Bazaar, tasted mouthwatering baklava, and watched the sun set behind the iconic Blue Mosque — a memory I’ll treasure forever.

Highlight of the trip: A peaceful evening cruise along the Bosphorus Strait, watching Istanbul’s skyline light up.

📝 Travel Tips for Istanbul

1. Layover

A short stop between connecting flights, usually in a different city, before reaching your final destination.

2. Jet Lag

A temporary sleep disorder caused by traveling across multiple time zones, disrupting your body’s internal clock.

3. All-Inclusive

A type of vacation package where meals, drinks, activities, and accommodations are included in the upfront price.

4. Backpacking

A style of budget travel that involves carrying all essentials in a backpack and often includes staying in hostels or budget lodgings.

5. Red-Eye Flight

An overnight flight that arrives early in the morning, typically named for the tired, bloodshot eyes of travelers.

6. Visa

An official authorization added to a passport that allows you to enter, stay, or work in a specific country for a set time.

7. Hostel

A low-cost accommodation, often with shared rooms and bathrooms, popular among young or budget-conscious travelers.

8. Cultural Immersion

Actively engaging with the local customs, food, language, and people of a destination to experience it authentically.
